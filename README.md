# devharsh.me

Source for my personal website, **[www.devharsh.me](https://www.devharsh.me)**. A fast static site built with plain HTML and CSS (no build step), hosted on GitHub Pages.

## About

With a Ph.D. in Cybersecurity and CISSP certification, I bring a unique blend of academic rigor and practical engineering experience to building secure systems and solving complex problems. My core research interests lie in applied cryptography and privacy-preserving machine learning. My career highlights include work in fully homomorphic encryption, where I proposed a novel probabilistic polynomial approximation approach using a perceptron and demonstrated its feasibility. As an IEEE Senior Member, I am highly focused on pursuing research and leadership roles situated at the critical juncture of cyber security, machine learning, and software engineering.

As a Senior Software Engineer at Philips and Senior Applications Engineer at Oracle, I have honed my skills across various programming languages, including C, C++, C#, R, Python, and Perl, applying them to academic and industrial challenges. I thrive as a self-motivated and adaptable remote contributor, valuing independent work and collaborative team engagement.

Beyond my engineering contributions, I am passionate about sharing knowledge and fostering the next generation of cybersecurity professionals. As a Guest Lecturer at Rashtriya Raksha University and a full-time Lecturer at Bowie State University, I have taught a variety of systems and security courses, specializing in systems programming and applied cryptography. I aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and practical education to cultivate a more secure digital future.

My dedication to advancing security extends to my research tenure as a Research Assistant at Stevens Institute of Technology and a Visiting Researcher at Télécom SudParis. In these roles, I focused on enhancing security and fairness in collaborative machine learning environments, leveraging my systems analysis and interpersonal communication strengths. Explore my research portfolio on [ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Devharsh-Trivedi/research), my technical writing on my [blog](https://com.puter.tips/), and more at [www.devharsh.me](https://www.devharsh.me).
